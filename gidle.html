(G)I-DLE is a girl group with 5 members that consists of Soyeon, Miyeon, Minnie, Yuqi and Shuhua. The group debuted on May 2nd, 2018 and they are under Cube Entertainement. (G)I-DLE was a girl group with 6 members, but 1 member left, Soojin. She left on August 14th, 2021. The fandom name is 'Neverland' and the fandom colors are neon red and chic violet.
