Seventeen is a boy group with 13 members that consists of S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, Vernon, Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, DK, Seungkwan, Hoshi, Jun, The8 and Dino. Seventeen has 3 leaders, because there are 3 units: hip-hop-, vocal- and performance-unit. They debuted on May 16th, 2015, with the mini album 17 Carat and the title track Adore You. The fandom name is 'Carat' and they're under Pledis Entertainement. Seventeen official fan colors are Rose Quartz and Serenity.
