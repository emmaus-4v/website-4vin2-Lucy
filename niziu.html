NiziU is a Japanese girl group with 9 members that consists of Mako, Rio, Maya, Riku, Ayaka, Mayuka, Rima, Miihi and Nina. . They debuted on December 2nd, 2020. the girl group is under JYP Entertainement and Sony Music Entertainement. NiziU is formed by a survival show called ' Nizi Project'. The fandom name is 'WithU' and the fandom color is a few colors from the rainbow. NiziU means: Rainbow will bring people together and they will shine like that. All members are representing a color.
